I have built an open-source library of explorable explanations for
Austrian economics called **Praxeos** (praxeos.org). The first module,
*The Halving Garden*, renders every Bitcoin block from genesis to tip as
a small Haeckel-style botanical organism on a Hilbert curve, arranged
into the four halving epochs as illuminated-manuscript pages.

*The Bitcoin Standard* is one of the project's primary sources — the
stock-to-flow argument and the Mengerian framing of Bitcoin as a
digital realisation of commodity money are load-bearing on the module's
essay, which cites it directly.
